Storyboard

SocialGov helps bringing citizens, executives and legislatives together to communicate and engage each other to bring transparent governance.  Legislatives can post any development ideas and have citizens debate on them to get unbiased view on the idea.  

Citizens can find city financial transactions, executives and legislative hierarchical contacts to bring transparency in the system.

Every user on this platform will have a specific role which gives them specific responsibilities and rights to do activities on the platform.

Citizens can post development ideas and city level problems which can be visible to legislatives and executives to work on. Executives can post any notifications and events happening in the city to empower citizens with the information.
 	

Eveyone can view the activity happening in the city and have rights to make decisions in their own city, not in other cities. 

It is our hope that we will find balanced governance and harmony in your city.
