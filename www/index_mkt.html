socialGov is an ardent effort by enthusiastic engineers to empower the citizens of a nation. We provide tools to bring all pillars of a nation (citizens, executives, legislature and civil society) together to communicate effectively and engage each other in governance, making it a true Democracy.

We are piloting in Sri Krishna Patnam and Dowlaiswaram villages in Andhra Pradesh, India to learn more about intricacies of Democracy.

If you are from any of these two villages, please install android app to be part of the pilot and share your feedback.
