SocialGov platform will bring citizens, executives, legislatives, journalists and social activist together to communicate and engage each other to bring transparence governance. Citizens can find executives and legislative hierarchical contacts and city financial transactions to bring transparency in the system.

We are piloting in few villages of Rajahmundry rural and Rajanagaram mandals in Andhra Pradesh, India to learn more about intricacies of Democracy.

If you belong any of these two villages, please install android app to be part of the pilot and share your feedback.
