socialGov is an ardent effort by enthusiastic engineers to empower the citizens of a nation. We are piloting in Sri Krishna Patnam and Dowlaiswaram villages in Andhra Pradesh, India to learn more about intricacies of Democracy.

If you belong any of these two villages, please install android app to be part of the pilot and share your feedback.
